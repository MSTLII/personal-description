Professional Background

Since 2004, I have consulted internationally as a specialist in cultural insight and semiotics for brand innovation & optimization, creating immersive experiences for media, luxury, fashion, transport, and consumer goods clients.

sensorial branding innovation

I have led global trends studies on developments in gender, beauty, luxury, premium-ness, health, American-ness and travel to inform future-facing creative strategies across touchpoints including new product design, transport and retail interiors packaging and user interface design.
My work has been used to inform creative strategies for fragrance & flavour innnovation, to colour, materials & lighting selection, to visual and verbal cues in broadcast and online media graphics, including gestures and vocal inflection and wardrobe for media personalities.

media & content strategy

For a decade, I have conducted genre specific studies tracking trends in original content, premium content, non fiction and reality television that have been syndicated for major American media, entertainment and news clients across traditional telelvision and streaming content.
